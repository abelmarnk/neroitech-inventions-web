Overview

Nigerian Solana startups are transforming finance by leveraging Solana’s high-throughput, low-cost blockchain to address challenges like currency devaluation and unreliable banking. Supported by Superteam Nigeria, these projects showcase strong product-market fit in a tough market. Key Insights

  • NectarFi achieved a 900% increase in transaction volume from June to August 2025, driven by its user-friendly savings platform.
  • Ribh processed $10.5M in cross-border payments, addressing liquidity gaps for SMEs with zero marketing spend.
  • PAJ CASH saw a 275% increase in transaction volume, focusing on seamless crypto-to-fiat conversions.
  • Cryptonia grew from $5M in October 2024 to $6M in July 2025, a 20% increase, showcasing rapid adoption for stablecoin-to-Naira transactions.
  • These startups leverage Solana’s low-cost, high-throughput infrastructure to solve real-world financial pain points in Nigeria.
